About Me

After having worked for a Republican Congressman and on a Democratic campaign, Dane realized that both parties are ill-equipped to deal with our nation's problems. Dane worked with a number of organizations in the political reform space before joining The Centrist Project in 2015. In his current role, Dane manages the Grassroots, Digital, and Founding Member programs.
